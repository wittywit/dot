Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the Dot Day Planner app.

Information We Collect

- Google Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we request access to your Google Calendar to read and write events. We do not store your Google account credentials.
- Calendar Data: The app can read your calendar events and create new events on your behalf. No calendar data is stored on our servers; all data is accessed directly from Google and used only within your browser.

How We Use Your Information

- To display your calendar events in the app.
- To allow you to create, update, or delete events in your Google Calendar.
- To improve your experience with the day planner.

Data Storage

- All your data is stored locally in your browser or in your Google account. We do not store or process your data on any external server.

Third-Party Services

- The app uses Google Calendar API to access your calendar data. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@wittywit.com.
